Welcome to Blackjack! The rules of this game are very simple:

  1. You and the computer will be dealt two cards each.
  2. You can choose to "Hit" (draw a card) or "Stand" (end your turn).
  3. The first card of computer will be hidden until you choose to "Stand".
  4. The player that has cards which sum is closer to, but not above 21 wins the hand.
Press the "Start" button to begin.
